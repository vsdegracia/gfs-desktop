Great Foods Solutions

Great Food Solutions (GFS) was established in 2002 as an integrated food service division of San Miguel Pure Foods Company, a derivative of San Miguel Corporation's acquisition of Pure Foods Corporation. The fusion of these two companies made both organizations stronger and a force to beat in the industry. Today, GFS is now the country's largest foodservice organization and is one of the most preferred partners. It manages key clients nationwide including major players in the quick service and full service restaurants, hotels, convenience stores, caterers, commissaries, cafes and bakeshops. The company offers a complete line of food products and services to meet the various needs of both individual and food service customers, chain or non-chain. GFS currently offers more than 1500 household SKU (Stock Keeping Units) from customized to regular products of fresh and processed meats, dairy, condiments, flour, fats and oils making it the most impressive foodservice supplier in terms of product range. Aside from food products, GFS is also dedicated in providing total business solutions from menu ideation to customized product development, product application and other value-added services.

Safety, quality and freshness are the priorities of the organization and ensuring those is the team of Food Protection Managers (Sales, Product Development and Marketing Teams) certified by the NRAEF (National Restaurants Association Educational Foundation, USA) and by ServSafe‚©, the industry's leading food safety training and certification program provider ensuring that products represent the words safety and quality. The distribution of GFS' products are guaranteed with their ISO-certified accredited plants that meet international standards, where customers are assured that the food they enjoy are fresh.

Knowing what the market wants and needs are the reason why GFS continuously gain ground and maintain to be on top of the food service suppliers in the country.
